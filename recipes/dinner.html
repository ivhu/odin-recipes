Korean-Style Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 bundle Korean Fresh Noodle
  • 1 egg
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1 tsp soy sauce, vinegar, Lao Gan Ma

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to a boil.
  2. Put 1 bundle of noodles and 1 egg into pot and boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Put noodles and egg into bowl (no soup).
  4. Slice 1 jalapeno and place into bowl.
  5. Pour 1 tsp of each ingredient into bowl: soy sauce, vinegar, Lao Gan Ma.
  6. Mix and enjoy!
