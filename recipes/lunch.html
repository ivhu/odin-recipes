Homemade Ramyun

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Shin Ramyun
  • 1 mini cucumber
  • 1 cup grape tomatoes
  • 1 pack nori

Steps

  1. Put 1 pack of ramyun in bowl.
  2. Pour hot water into bowl and cover for 3 minutes.
  3. Wash 1 mini cucumber & 1 cup of grape tomatoes and place into bowl.
  4. Place 1 pack of nori into bowl.
  5. Wait until bowl cools a bit and enjoy!
